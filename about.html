About Me

Hi, I'm Michaela Valik! I'm a nutritionist, a certified Czech Nutrition Coach (MSMT), and most importantly, a mom. Originally from the Czech Republic, I now live on the beautiful Gold Coast with my husband and our lively 3-year-old daughter.

Life as a busy mom has taught me so much about finding balance—juggling work, family, and self-care isn't always easy, but it's possible. I've learned how to create simple, practical strategies that fit real life, and now I'm here to help you do the same.

For me, health isn't just about food. It's about feeling good in your body, enjoying what you eat, and building habits that work for your life—not against it. Whether it's walking on the beach with my daughter, cooking wholesome meals for my family, or carving out moments to recharge, I know that the little things add up to make a big difference.

I'm passionate about helping you feel your best—whether that means losing weight, boosting your energy, or just finding more joy and ease in your daily life. My approach is simple, compassionate, and tailored to you because no two journeys are the same.
